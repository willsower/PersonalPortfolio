Healthy Eats Prototype

4 Person Team

An Android app for healthy and affordable recipes. Healthy Eats will generate healthy meals for themselves and their families based on their budget and the ingredients they already have. By having this application display this information, individuals and their families can benefit from a healthier diet, which in the long run can lead to a healthier life. We are planning to achieve SDG goal three, “Good Health and Wellbeing.” This goal will be achieved since we are directly impacting the user and their family in eating a healthier and nutritious meal.

Date

September 2020 - December 2020

Languages & Tools Used
