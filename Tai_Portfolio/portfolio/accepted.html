AcceptED Dashboard Project

Contracted Project

Lead front-end developer and part time backend developer at AcceptED. Consulted in creating AcceptED's dashboard for Students, Education Consultants, and Administration users.

AcceptED is an organization based in California that is striving to close the education gap. This dashboard is to have High School students get paird up with Education Consultants who will mentor and give advice into several universities. The developer team works with the design team and leadership for this project.
